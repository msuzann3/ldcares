L&D Cares Privacy Policy

At L&D Cares, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. When you share your name, email address, or other contact details with us, such as when registering for a webinar or event, we use that information solely for the following purposes:

  • To send you emails regarding any events or webinars you may have registered for
  • To connect you with a mentor or mentee

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time by clicking the link in our emails or contacting us directly.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is used, please email us.
